![gameplay.gif](///raw/503/z/30204.gif)


# Controls:
---
- ** Left click ** or ** Enter ** to attack
- ** A,D ** to move
- ** S ** key to enter Zombie suit

![ss5.png](///raw/503/z/304e6.png)


# Game:
---

Assemble zombie suit to help the scientist and ** keep it alive** .

:small_blue_diamond: Platformer

:small_blue_diamond: Collect parts to complete the zombie

:small_blue_diamond: Beat'em up


![ss3.png](///raw/503/z/30507.png)


# About the Team:
---

[LandoSystems](https://lando.systems) is a group of jammers from Madison, Wisconsin that varies in size from 2 to 12 people. This is the team's 26th Ludum Dare game!! :grinning:
